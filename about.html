Our history

The history of MON MOU began in 2018, when the founders of the brand Zhanna and Oksana combined experience, knowledge and efforts to create a comfortable and useful product for hair care. At first it was a personal question, and there was no question of mass production. But over time, it became clear that the development would benefit other girls as well ...

Jeanne has thick and long hair. Since childhood, she most of all did not like to wash her hair and dry her hair. As an adult, Jeanne began to appreciate what nature gave her, but washing her hair remained an unloved occupation. And the ever-falling towels and, as a result, a wet back added reasons for this dislike. Began a long search for ways to make shampooing easier and more comfortable.

All the proposed options were made of microfiber, unpleasant to the touch, besides, Jeanne's long and thick hair did not fit into any turban. Then Zhanna turned to Oksana with a request to make a turban of a suitable size for her. The moment Jeanne's needs combined with Oksana's experience, magic happened.
